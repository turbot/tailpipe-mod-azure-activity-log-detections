## Overview

Detect deletions of Azure VPN connections. VPN connections are essential for secure communication between on-premises networks and Azure, and their deletion can disrupt connectivity and compromise operations. Monitoring these deletions ensures network stability and prevents unauthorized or accidental removal of critical connections.
