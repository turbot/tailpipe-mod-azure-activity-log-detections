## Overview

Detect restore operations for secrets in Azure Key Vault. Restoring a secret can reintroduce previously deleted or modified sensitive information, potentially impacting security or operational workflows. Monitoring these events ensures proper oversight of secret management and prevents unauthorized restorations.
